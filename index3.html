Social Media Platforms

Facebook
  • - Founded: February 4, 2004, by Mark Zuckerberg and fellow Harvard students
  • Current Owner: Meta Platforms Inc. (formerly Facebook Inc.)
  • - Purpose: A social networking site to connect people, share updates, photos, videos, and more -
  • - Users: Over 3 billion monthly active users as of June 2025
  • - Global Reach: Available in over 100 languages and used worldwide
    • Instagram
  • - Launched: October 6, 2010, by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger
  • - Owned by: Meta Platforms Inc. (formerly Facebook Inc.)
  • - Purpose: A photo and video-sharing social networking app designed for visual storytelling
  • - Users: Over 2 billion monthly active users worldwide
    •

    WhatsApp
  • - Founded: February 2009 by Brian Acton and Jan Koum
  • - Owned by: Meta Platforms Inc. (formerly Facebook Inc.)
  • - Users: Over 2 billion active users globally
    •




    Twitter
  • - Founded: March 2013 by brothers Nikolai and Pavel Durov, who previously created VKontakte (VK), Russia’s largest social network
  • - Headquarters: Legal base in the British Virgin Islands, operational center in Dubai, UAE
  • - Users: Over 1 billion monthly active users as of March 2025, with India leading in user count



    • Snapchat
  • - Launched: September 2011 by Evan Spiegel, Bobby Murphy, and Reggie Brown
  • - Core Idea: Share photos and videos (“Snaps”) that disappear after being viewed
  • - Owned by: Snap Inc.
    •


    Threads
  • - Launched: July 5, 2023
  • - Developed by: Meta Platforms Inc. (parent company of Facebook and Instagram)
  • - Purpose: A microblogging app designed to rival Twitter (now X), focused on public conversations


    • YouTube
  • - Founded: February 14, 2005, by Chad Hurley, Steve Chen, and Jawed Karim
  • - Owned by: Google LLC (acquired in 2006 for $1.65 billion)
  • - Users: Over 2.7 billion monthly active users as of January 2024


    • Telegram
  • - Founded: March 2013 by brothers Nikolai and Pavel Durov, creators of the Russian social network VK
  • - Headquarters: Operational center in Dubai, UAE; legal domicile in the British Virgin Islands -
  • - Users: Over 1 billion monthly active users as of March 2025, with India leading in user count
